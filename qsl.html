QSL

QSL and log confirmations

I am happy to confirm contacts. The easiest way is via QRZ Logbook, backed up by a quick email so I can match everything correctly. I also use eQSL.

How to get a confirmation

  1. Log the QSO in your QRZ Logbook or eQSL.
  2. Email me the details, or send a screenshot or photo of your log entry.
  3. I will match it in my log, add a comment, and confirm.
Important note

When I log a contact, I generally send the QSO details to your QRZ email address to make confirmation easy. I will always add a comment to the log.

What to include
  • Your callsign
  • UTC date and time
  • Band or frequency, or network used
  • Mode used, for example SSB, FM, Digital, EchoLink, AllStar
  • Signal report if available, optional
